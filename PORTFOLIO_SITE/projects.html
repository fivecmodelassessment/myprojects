The Five C Model Assesment - Still in BETA

Idea

The idea was born in the head of A.R. Jardine few years ago. And it was about time to get a more usable and acsesible to busineses and companyes.

Creation

We had a meeting to clear out "what is the main goal". After answering this question, we started work on the project. I created the front-end part and a developer from the UK created the Back-End part. Constant communication via mail and face to face resulted in the project.

The Model

The Five C Model is the result of a ten year collaboration with The Straumann Group, Basel, Switzerland. I was originally commissioned to develop an educational product for their end user implantologist. The Model provides a context for holistic insight and improvement of both business profitability and patient care standards.

A.R. Jardine

ARJardine is an international business advisory service that specialises in people and systems development. A R Jardine (the person) remains curious, passionate and driven. The mission is to produce high quality outcomes.
